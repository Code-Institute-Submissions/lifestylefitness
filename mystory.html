My Story

January 2017

The start of my journey was tough

Living in denial was easier than facing up to the reality. Until one day I was confronted by my physician about my recent bloodwork. He warned me that if I was to continue my unhealthy habits I would not live past 56. This was a wake up call as my own father passed at the age of just 49 from a heart attack. I knew I needed to change but also had to involve professionals.

June 2017

The first successes and regained confidence

Truly successful people learn and move on precisely because they know how to forgive themselves first. I realised that it's just as bad to be mean to yourself as it is to be mean to others. The moment I realized this and learned to forgive yourself was the moment I had given myself permission to rise again and make things happen. Things started to shift.

February 2020

The new me after a lot of hard work and persistance

My entire life changed because of these crucial moments. I have since been able to own up to my mental illness, help people to transform online, become a personak trainer, compete in the regional championships of crossfit, meet a woman who I fell in love with, and can safely that I regret nothing.
